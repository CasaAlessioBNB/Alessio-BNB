About us

Welcome to Casa Alessio B&B, a simple but elegant place in the heart of the city of Langhe. An ancient house that is now a place of relaxation. It will be our great pleasure to take care of you, welcome you and let you know the most beautiful things to see in Langhe. Located in the town of Barolo our B&B is a private place of luxury, silence and relaxation. On the ground floor you will find our entrance hall, a comfortable lounge with fireplace, the kitchen and the our guests rooms.

If you are looking for something a little different, and possibly, even a little bit cheaper than a hotel, we would recommend Casa Alessio B&B. From our delicious breakfast, stunning place to our hosts ensuring that any information given is up to date and helpful. See you soon!
